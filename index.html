About Our Club

Our History

Founded in 2005, Banyu Hilir has grown from a small local club to a nationally recognized swimming program with over 500 active members and numerous championship titles.

Our Achievements

We've produced 15 national champions, 3 Olympic qualifiers, and won over 200 regional competitions. Our swimmers consistently rank in the top 10% nationally.

Our Coaches

Our team of 8 professional coaches includes former Olympians and NCAA champions, bringing world-class expertise to swimmers of all levels.
